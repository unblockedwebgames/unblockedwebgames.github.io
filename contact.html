Frequently Asked Questions

How can I suggest a new game to be added? +
We're always looking for new games to add! Please use the contact form above and select "Game Suggestion" as the subject. Include the name of the game and why you think it would be a good fit for our website.
A game isn't working properly. What should I do? +
If you're experiencing issues with a specific game, please contact us using the form above. Select "Game Issue/Bug Report" as the subject and include the game name, description of the problem, and what browser/device you're using.
Are the games on your website really free? +
Yes! All games on Unblocked Web Games are completely free to play. There are no hidden fees, subscriptions, or in-app purchases required to enjoy our games.
Can I use your website on my school computer? +
Our website is designed to be accessible in various environments, but we recommend checking with your school's IT policies before using our site on school computers. We are not responsible for any consequences of using our website in restricted environments.
How often do you add new games? +
We regularly update our game collection with new titles. While there's no fixed schedule, we typically add several new games each month. Follow us on social media to stay updated on new additions!
